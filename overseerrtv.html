overseerrTV

App details

overseerrTV brings the discovery experience of overseerr (and jellyseerr) to your AppleTV. Browse series and movies, make requests, view trailers and search. All the benefits of overseerr from the comfort of your couch. If you don't know what overseerr or jellyseerr are then this app is probably not for you.
